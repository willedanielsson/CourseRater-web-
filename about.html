CourseRater

CourseRater is a free service which allows students to rate courses at their university. With a selection of thousands of universities from all over the world, all students can contribute.

Idea

The idea behind CourseRater is that students can evaluate and rate courses that they have taken. The easy rating system simplifies the evaluation progress as students rate each part of the course separately and can also leave comments for every part of the course. The ratings and comments are anonymous and the overall rating of the course is displayed for everyone.

Results

It can sometimes be difficult to understand the content and quality of a course by reading the information provided by the university. Unfortunately, students often realize that a course is not their cup of tea when it's too late or troublesome to change.
We hope that the ratings and comments from students that have already taken the course will aid other students in their choices. It is a students-help-students relation with CourseRater as an intermediary.

Consequences

It can take a long time until a crappy course is changed or removed as long as unknowing students is taking it. Our ambition is that we can contribute to change these courses by providing the ratings from earlier students. This can be achieved since the course administrator will be forced to modify the course if the attendance is rapidly decreasing. The administrator can report any changes in the course to us which in turn will be displayed for students.

Contributions

Since it is impossible for us to add all of the courses at every university, we need the users to help us out. If there is no registered courses from your university on CourseRater, add as many as you can and spread the word. We need users to supply us with data and get the ball rolling!

Why do I need to be logged in?

Everyone can view ratings and comments regarding a course. However, you have to be logged in as a user to be able to add a course or rate an existing course. The registration takes only a couple of seconds and we require you to be logged in so that we can remove users that try to exploit the system. However, your ratings of courses will not displayed on the site.
